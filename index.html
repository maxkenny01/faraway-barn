Frequently asked

Where is Faraway Barn?

In quiet countryside near Berkeley, South Gloucestershire — around ten minutes from the M5 (Junction 14). The exact address is shared once your booking is confirmed.

How many guests does it sleep?

Up to four. There's a king-size bedroom, plus a pull-out king-size day bed in the living area.

Is the barn completely private?

Yes — it's a fully self-contained annexe with its own private entrance, garden and parking, set in three private acres of grounds.

Is there parking?

Yes, private parking for up to three cars.

Is there WiFi?

Yes, superfast WiFi throughout.

Are pets allowed?

We're not able to accept pets at the moment.

What's there to do nearby?

Berkeley Castle is about five minutes away, WWT Slimbridge and Thornbury around fifteen, and Bristol roughly thirty. The Cotswolds and the Forest of Dean are both easy day trips — see On the doorstep above.

How do I book?

Bookings are made through Airbnb, so every stay is covered by secure payments and AirCover protection. Use Check availability to see live dates.
